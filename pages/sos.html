🛡 Safety Tips for Tourists

📱Keep your phone charged at all times and save this app's SOS shortcut on your home screen.
📍Always share your location and itinerary with someone you trust before exploring new areas.
💰Carry a small amount of cash and keep your valuables (wallet, passport) secure and out of sight.
🕐Avoid deserted areas after dark. Stick to well-lit, busy tourist areas in the evenings.
🚗Only use registered taxis or Ola/Uber. Note the vehicle number before boarding.
🏥Nearest hospital: Bombay Hospital (0731-2444444), MY Hospital (0731-2433780)
